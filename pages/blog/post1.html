Top 10 Marvel Comics of 2024

Share: 📘 🐦 📌 📧

The Marvel Universe continues to expand and evolve with incredible storytelling, breathtaking artwork, and unforgettable characters. 2024 has been a phenomenal year for Marvel comics, and we've curated a list of the absolute best issues and series you need to read.

10. Amazing Spider-Man #42

The latest arc in Peter Parker's life takes an unexpected turn as a new villain emerges from his past. Zeb Wells and John Romita Jr. deliver another masterpiece that reminds us why Spider-Man remains Marvel's flagship character.

Why it made the list: The character development and emotional depth in this issue set a new standard for superhero comics.

9. X-Men: Rise of Krakoa

Jonathan Hickman's vision for the X-Men reaches new heights as the mutant nation faces its greatest challenge yet. This issue features the return of a classic villain and sets up the next major crossover event.

Why it made the list: The political intrigue and world-building make this essential reading for any X-Men fan.

8. Thor: God of Storms #7

Donny Cates continues his epic run on Thor with a storyline that explores the God of Thunder's connection to the cosmos. The art by Nic Klein is absolutely breathtaking, with double-page spreads that demand to be seen.

Why it made the list: The cosmic scale and mythological elements create an epic feel that only Thor can deliver.

7. Doctor Strange: Multiverse of Madness

Following the events of the movie, this series delves deeper into the mysteries of the multiverse. Jed MacKay's writing perfectly captures Strange's voice, while the art team creates psychedelic visuals that push the boundaries of comic art.

Why it made the list: The creative use of the comic medium to depict multiversal travel is innovative and exciting.

6. Black Panther: Wakanda Forever #3

John Ridley's take on T'Challa explores the weight of leadership and the legacy of the Black Panther. This issue features a touching tribute to Chadwick Boseman while moving the character forward in meaningful ways.

Why it made the list: The emotional depth and cultural significance make this more than just a superhero comic.

5. Fantastic Four: Reborn

Marvel's First Family returns to their roots in this spectacular series by Ryan North. The focus on scientific exploration and family dynamics brings back the magic that made the Fantastic Four great.

Why it made the list: It captures the spirit of exploration and wonder that defined the early Fantastic Four comics.

4. Daredevil: Devil's Reign

Chip Zdarsky's run on Daredevil reaches its climax as Wilson Fisk's reign as Mayor comes to a head. Marco Checchetto's art is cinematic and powerful, capturing every moment of Matt Murdock's struggle.

Why it made the list: The courtroom drama mixed with superhero action creates a unique reading experience.

3. Moon Knight: Fist of Khonshu

Building on the success of the Disney+ series, this new run explores Marc Spector's multiple personalities in fascinating ways. The art shifts styles to represent different personalities, creating a visually stunning experience.

Why it made the list: The innovative art style and psychological depth make this a must-read.

2. Captain America: Sentinel of Liberty

Ta-Nehisi Coates continues his thought-provoking exploration of what it means to be Captain America in modern America. This series tackles relevant social issues while maintaining the heroic spirit of Steve Rogers.

Why it made the list: The intelligent writing and social commentary elevate this beyond typical superhero fare.

1. Avengers: Forever Saga

Jason Aaron's epic Avengers run concludes with a storyline that brings together heroes from across time and space. The final issue features a battle so massive it literally reshapes the Marvel Universe.

Why it made the list: The scope and ambition of this story make it the definitive Marvel event of 2024.

Honorable Mentions

  • She-Hulk: Attorney at Law - Perfect blend of humor and legal drama
  • Ms. Marvel: New Mutant - Kamala Khan joins the X-Men
  • Ghost-Spider: Across the Multiverse - Gwen Stacy's continuing adventures
  • Wolverine: Weapon X-Files - A noir take on everyone's favorite mutant

Final Thoughts

2024 has proven that Marvel Comics continues to innovate and entertain. Whether you're a longtime fan or new to comics, there's never been a better time to dive into the Marvel Universe. Head over to our shop to pick up these amazing issues and start your collection today!
